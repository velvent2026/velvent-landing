Retail

The retail industry connects producers and consumers by offering products through physical stores and digital platforms, focusing on convenience, pricing, and customer experience.

Retail Segment

  • Sales channels: Physical stores, online platforms, mobile commerce.
  • Customer experience: Store layout, personalization, loyalty programs.
  • Operations: Inventory management, logistics, supplier coordination.
  • Technology: POS systems, digital payments, analytics, CRM tools.

Retail plays a vital role in everyday consumer life by adapting to evolving shopping behaviors and market trends.
