Other Businesses – Detailed Overview

Other Businesses represent diversified commercial activities that operate independently or complement core sectors such as retail, fashion, services, and food & beverages.

These ventures help organizations expand revenue streams, reduce risk, and explore innovation-driven opportunities. By entering niche markets and emerging industries, businesses can achieve long-term scalability and competitive advantage.
